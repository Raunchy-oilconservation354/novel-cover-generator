novel-cover-generator helps you make novel and book covers from text. It is made for web novels, romance stories, fantasy tales, and wuxia stories. You give it a title, a story style, and a few cover ideas. It helps you create a cover that fits your story.

Use it for:

- Novel covers
- Book covers
- Web novel art
- Romance cover art
- Fantasy cover art
- Wuxia cover art

## 🧭 How to use it

### 1. Open the app

Start the program from the file you downloaded. If you placed it on your desktop, double-click the app icon.

### 2. Enter your story details

Type in:

- The novel title
- The story genre
- The main character type
- The mood you want
- Any scene ideas you want on the cover

Short, clear text works best. For example:

- A cold sword master in moonlight
- A romance story with a red rose theme
- A fantasy hero standing on a cliff
- A wuxia master in a bamboo forest

### 3. Choose a cover style

Pick a style that fits your story. Common choices include:

- Dark fantasy
- Soft romance
- Action scene
- Elegant fantasy
- Martial arts
- Modern web novel

### 4. Generate the cover

Click the button that creates the image. The app will build a cover based on your text and style choices.

### 5. Save the result

When you like the cover, save it to your computer. You can use it for your novel page, book listing, or personal project.

## 🖼️ Cover types you can make

### Fantasy covers

Use these for magic stories, swords, kingdoms, and epic quests. Good prompts often include:

- Ancient castles
- Glowing power
- Dragons
- Magic circles
- Stormy skies

### Romance covers

Use these for love stories and emotional scenes. Good prompts often include:

- A couple in soft light
- Flowers
- Sunset scenes
- Warm colors
- Gentle expressions

### Wuxia covers

Use these for martial arts stories and classic Chinese action tales. Good prompts often include:

- Swords
- Bamboo forests
- Mountain peaks
- Flowing robes
- Calm, strong poses

### Web novel covers

Use these for online fiction and serial stories. Good prompts often include:

- Bold title space
- Clear main figure
- Strong color contrast
- Simple scene layout

## 🛠️ Tips for better results

- Keep your title short
- Use one main subject
- Add a clear genre
- Pick a color mood
- Avoid too many scene details at once
- Try a few versions before you choose the final cover

Good input gives better output. A simple prompt often works better than a long one.
